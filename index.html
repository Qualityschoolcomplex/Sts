Guide

Android blocks direct Settings access from browsers. Here are two ways to get there:

Option A — Fastest

  1. Swipe down your notification bar
  2. Tap the gear icon ⚙️

Option B — Via browser icon

  1. Go to your home screen
  2. Long-press your browser icon
  3. Tap App info

Android doesn't allow browsers to open App Permissions directly. Go there manually:

Direct path

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Apps
  3. Find and tap your browser (Chrome)
  4. Tap Permissions

Shortcut

  1. Long-press your browser icon on the home screen
  2. Tap App info → Permissions

Android doesn't allow browsers to open Accounts settings directly. Go there manually:

Android 8+

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Accounts (or Users & accounts / Passwords & accounts)

Samsung devices

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Accounts and backup → Manage accounts
